Flood Victims Camp Fund

This year due to an unexpected heavy rainfall and rainstroms in Maharashtra, level of rivers suddenly increased at very high rate and also all dams were flooded. Because of this many places like Chiplun, Sangli, Kolhapur had to face flash-floods. So many people became victims of this flood, many lost their homes their families,shops,cattles almost everything.

Our Hand of Help Flood Victims Campaign is helping these peoples for everything we can along with many other Teams and CSR Teams. We are providing them Shelter, Food, Hospital Assistance. We need your help to raise Funds for our Campaing so that help to these people never stop.
